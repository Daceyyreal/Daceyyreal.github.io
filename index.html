Undergraduate Thesis

Beyond Naive INT8: Adaptive Post-Training Compression for 3D Gaussian Splatting

First author · under review, ICCA 2026 · trained on Kaggle T4 · scenes: Garden, Bicycle, Vase
Four-stage pipeline — adaptive opacity pruning, outlier clipping, SH-degree reduction, quantization — applied to standard splatfacto checkpoints with no retraining.
Size–quality Pareto frontier across three scenes. Mixed precision holds quality while cutting ~74% of file size.

A scene-independent quality cliff turned out to be a quantization-range artifact. Morton Z-order sub-group ranges recover to within 0.08–0.66 dB of FP16, self-decodable from a sidecar.
